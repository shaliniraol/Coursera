<tr>
<td>featVariable</td>

</tr>
<tr>
<td>featJerk</td>

<tr>
<td>featMagnitude</td>
<td>Feature: Magnitude of the signals calculated using the Euclidean norm</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>featAxis</td>

</tr>

</td>
